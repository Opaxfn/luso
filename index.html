Frequently Asked Questions About
Dental Services

Are dental check-ups really necessary every six months?

Yes, regular dental check-ups help to catch potential issues early, such as cavities or gum disease, before they become more serious and expensive to treat.

How often should I get a professional teeth cleaning?

It is recommended to get a professional cleaning at least twice a year to prevent plaque buildup and maintain healthy gums.

Are dental treatments covered by insurance?

Many dental insurance plans cover preventive care, such as cleanings and x-rays, but coverage for other treatments varies. Contact your insurance provider for specific details.

What should I do if I experience dental pain?

If you are experiencing dental pain, it's best to contact us for an appointment as soon as possible. Ignoring the pain could lead to more serious dental issues.

What options do I have for cosmetic dentistry?

We offer various cosmetic dental treatments such as teeth whitening, veneers, and bonding. Contact us to discuss which option is best suited for your smile.
